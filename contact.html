Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is your milk?

Our milk is collected daily from healthy cows and delivered within 6-12 hours of milking to ensure maximum freshness and nutrition.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery in Dhaka, Gazipur, and Narayanganj. Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the same day.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bKash, Nagad, and bank transfers. No advance payment required for regular deliveries.

Can I visit your farm?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors to see our cows and farm operations. Please call ahead to schedule a visit during business hours.
